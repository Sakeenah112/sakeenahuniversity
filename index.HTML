Welcome To Computer Science At UNISAK

The Department Of Computer Science at the University Of Sakeenah is a center for innovation, and is dedicated to providing student with a comprehensive and cutting-edge education in the rapidly evolving field of computing. Our department are leading researchers and experienced educators commited to fostering innovation and excellence.

Department Overview

We offer a range of undergraduate and graduate programs designed to equip students with the theoritical foundations and pactical skills necessary to succeed in diverse areas of Computer Science, including software development, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and data science.
